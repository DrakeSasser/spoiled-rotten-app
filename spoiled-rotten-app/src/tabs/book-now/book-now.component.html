Our Services

Playtime

Our indoor playroom offers a safe, climate-controlled space for year-round play, perfect for rain or shine. Designed to encourage socialization and reduce anxiety, our nurturing environment helps boost your dog’s happiness, health, and behavior—all under the watchful care of our attentive staff.

Boarding

Our spacious kennels are housed in rooms with just three kennels each, and no kennel doors face one another—helping reduce stress and create a calm, restful space. Each guest enjoys four walks a day, and you can personalize their stay with optional individual playtime or small group play sessions for extra enrichment.

Grooming

Our Fear Free certified groomers are trained to recognize stress signals and modify the grooming process to ensure your pet feels calm and comfortable every step of the way. In our cozy, pet-friendly grooming spaces, your dog will enjoy a clean, fresh, and pampered experience—tailored to their unique needs.
